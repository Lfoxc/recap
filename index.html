Demon Slayer

Kimetsu no yaiba

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who sets out to become a demon slayer in search of a cure after his family is slaughtered and his only living family member, his younger sister Nezuko, is turned into a demon.
